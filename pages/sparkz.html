About KARE:

Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education (KARE), formerly Arulmigu Kalasalingam College of Engineering, established in 1984, became a Deemed University in 2006. Located in the scenic Western Ghats of Tamilnadu, it offers UG, PG, and Ph.D. programs. Accredited 'A++' by NAAC and six UG programs by NBA, KARE is known for its pioneering B.Tech program for differently-abled students. It received DST funding for the National Center for Advanced Research in Discrete Mathematics and has state-of-the-art facilities, hostels, a swimming pool, and an auditorium, emphasizing holistic student development through various activities.

About Sparkz:

Sparkz, our vibrant inter-college event, is a celebration of diverse talents and creativity on March 1st and 2nd, 2024. From captivating dance performances to melodious singing, captivating photography, versatile artistic expressions, and engaging variety shows, Sparkz encompasses a spectrum of talent. The event also features entertaining and fun-filled games, adding an extra layer of excitement. With the inclusion of proshows, Sparkz promises a dynamic and entertaining platform for students to showcase their skills and share memorable moments. The fest will hold an audience of around 7000 students participants and will be a great opportunity to spread your brand around tamilnadu. Join us in this exhilarating extravaganza that brings the entire campus community together in the spirit of talent, creativity, and joy.
